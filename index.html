About EMP PARTS CO., LTD.

Founded in 2012, EMP PARTS is a vertically integrated manufacturer and exporter of heavy equipment spare parts based in Shenyang, China. We operate in-house piston and gearbox factories, delivering OEM-grade components.

Our proprietary brands — PME (engine parts) and FOPFUJI (hydraulic units) — set the benchmark for aftermarket quality.
