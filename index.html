About Us

About EMP PARTS CO., LTD.

Founded in 2012, EMP PARTS is a vertically integrated manufacturer and exporter of heavy equipment spare parts based in Shenyang, China. We operate in-house piston and gearbox factories, delivering OEM-grade components through certified production lines.

Our proprietary brands — PME (precision engine parts) and FOPFUJI (hydraulic units) — set the benchmark for aftermarket quality. Every unit passes rigorous pressure and dimensional testing before shipment.

From our 12,000m² central warehouse we maintain $10M+ in ready stock, enabling 48-hour emergency response anywhere in China and rapid global air or sea freight.
