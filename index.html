About EMP PARTS CO., LTD.

Founded in 2012, EMP PARTS is a vertically integrated manufacturer with in-house piston and gearbox factories. We deliver OEM-grade components through certified production lines. Our proprietary brands PME (engine parts) and FOPFUJI (hydraulic units) exemplify precision manufacturing standards.

Our 12,000m² central warehouse in Shenyang holds over $10M inventory, ensuring 48-hour emergency response across China. Serving 30+ countries, we provide complete aftermarket solutions for all major excavator brands including CAT, Komatsu, Hitachi, Doosan, Hyundai, Volvo, and SANY.
